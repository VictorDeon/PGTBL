Game rules

The rules of the game are simple:

  • The Peer review is necessary to evaluate the performance of your team colleague during the TBL process.
  • This evaluation is entirely anonymous, only the evaluated student can see the feedback and his name will not be shown.
  • The student has the option of leaving a comment for the evaluated colleague, it will be shown in their profile anonymously.
  • You'll have 100 points to distribute among the participants of your group.
  • The evaluation sum of all group members evaluating you will constitute your grade in the respective evaluation.
  • If the sum exceeds 100 points a grade bonus will occur, otherwise the grade will be the default evaluation mentioned before.
  • Example 01: In a team of 5 people, each member of the team gives you 20 points, resulting in a total of 100 points granting you maximum grade.
  • Example 02: In a team of 5 people, each member of the team gives you a specific number of points and the final sum is 110, in that case you'll get the maximum grade plus a bonus of 10 points, that's equal to the exceeding value past 100.
  • Example 03: In a team of 5 people, each member of the team gives you a specific number of points and the final sum is 80, then your grade will be calculated accordingly to that total points.
