{% trans 'Pair Review Rules' %}

{% trans 'The rules of the game are very simple:' %}

  • {% trans 'Peer evaluation is used to evaluate the performance of your teammates during TBL.' %}
  • {% blocktrans %} This evaluation is totally anonymous, only the evaluated student will receive the feedback and will not appear his name. {% endblocktrans %}
  • {% blocktrans %} The student can leave a comment for the evaluated colleague, this will appear in their profile in anonymous. {% endblocktrans %}
  • {% trans 'You will have 100 points to distribute among the participants in your group.' %}
  • {% blocktrans %} The sum of the evaluation of all the members of the group evaluating you will compose your grade in this evaluation. {% endblocktrans %}
  • {% blocktrans %} If the sum total of 100 points will have a bonus on the note, otherwise it will compose the score according to the score. {% endblocktrans %}
  • {% trans 'Example' %} 01: {% blocktrans %} In a team of 5 members, each member inserts 20 points into you, then adding everything will give you 100 points and you will get a maximum score. {% endblocktrans %}
  • {% trans 'Example' %} 02: {% blocktrans %} In a team of 5 members, each member inserts the score in you and the sum of the 110, soon you will receive the maximum score plus a bonus. {% endblocktrans %}
  • {% trans 'Example' %} 03: {% blocktrans %} In a team of 5 members, each member enters the score on you and the sum of the 80, then your score will be calculated according to that score. {% endblocktrans %}
